Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ridge work?

Ridge helps you plan your ski day by selecting multiple runs on the resort map. Our smart algorithm then calculates the fastest, most efficient route to complete all your selected runs, minimizing time spent on lifts and traversing.

Which resorts are supported?

Ridge currently supports select ski resorts, with new locations being added regularly. Check the app's resort selector to see available locations.

Does Ridge work offline?

Resort maps are stored locally on your device after download, allowing you to use basic navigation features offline. However, some features may require an internet connection.

Is Ridge a replacement for resort signage?

No. Ridge is a recreational navigation tool and should not replace official resort signage, maps, or safety equipment. Always prioritize local signage and resort rules.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Please email us at the support address above with details about the issue or feature request. We appreciate all feedback!

Does Ridge collect my personal data?

Ridge does not collect personal identifiers like your name, email, or phone number. We only collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
